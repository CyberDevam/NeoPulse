🎯 The Mission

Most productivity apps are bloated, require accounts, and send your data to the cloud. NeoPulse is different. Everything lives in your browser — instant, private, and zero friction.

  • No account required — open and start immediately
  • All data stored locally in your browser
  • No ads, no subscriptions, no tracking
  • Simple enough to use daily, powerful enough to impress

🧠 The Pomodoro Technique

Developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s, the Pomodoro Technique breaks work into focused intervals — traditionally 25 minutes — separated by short breaks. It combats mental fatigue and trains your brain to focus on demand.

  • 25-minute focus sessions minimize distraction
  • 5-min breaks restore attention and energy
  • 15-min long break after every 4 sessions
  • Track your streaks to build lasting habits
