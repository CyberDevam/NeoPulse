How NeoPulse Works

Get started in seconds — no signup, no friction.

1

Open Notes & Capture Ideas

Head to the Notes page and click "New Note". Give it a title, write your content, pick a color label and save. Notes are instantly stored in your browser.

2

Start a Pomodoro Session

Jump to Pomodoro, hit Start, and work for 25 minutes uninterrupted. The nav bar shows a live timer so you never lose track — even on other pages.

3

Take Your Break & Review

When the session ends, NeoPulse rings a gentle alert. Take your break, then check your session count and total focus time right in the app.
